## Hands Up Education

We are [Hands Up Education](https://hands-up-education.org/), a not-for-profit organisation and international community of practice,
creating and sharing high quality teaching resources.

Our creative, passionate and dedicated team are leading change and growth in the teaching of Latin and Classics.
